## Information Collection

We collect the following information:

- User profile (nickname, avatar) from WeChat/Alipay
- Videos uploaded for processing
- Usage logs and processing history
- Device information (model, OS version)

---

## Information Usage

Collected information is used to:

- Provide video processing services
- Process transactions and manage points
- Improve service quality
- Ensure platform security

---

## Information Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties except:

- Aliyun (for video processing)
- Payment platforms (WeChat Pay / Alipay)

See [Third-Party Sharing](../third-party-sharing.md) for details.

## Information Storage

- Data is stored on servers located in China
- Data retention: active account data is retained indefinitely
- Deleted account data is purged within 30 days
